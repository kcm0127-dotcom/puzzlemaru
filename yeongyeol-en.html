How to Play

Word Links rules

The 16 words hide four groups of four. Select four words and hit Submit. You can make up to 4 mistakes.

Each group has a difficulty: yellow is easiest, purple is trickiest. Watch out for trap words that seem to fit two groups.

If exactly three of your four are right, you'll be told you're "one away".
