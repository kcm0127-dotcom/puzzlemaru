## ④ 예비 — Reddit r/WebGames (영어, 넷째 주)

**Title**: I made a 3-player Gomoku where you fight two AIs that also fight each other

**Body**:

Classic Gomoku (five-in-a-row) but with three players: you and two AIs.
The fun part: when someone makes a four-in-a-row, one of the OTHER two has to block it —
and the AIs will happily let each other die. Sneaking your line through while they
fight each other is a completely different feeling from 1v1.

No install, no sign-up, runs in the browser (EN toggle in the nav):
https://puzzlemaru.xyz/omok3.html?from=reddit

There's also a reverse mode where making five-in-a-row LOSES,
and an online 1v1 you can play with a friend via room code.
